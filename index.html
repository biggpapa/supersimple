Our Foundation

Why Embrace Bharat

India's strength lies in its people. We exist to unlock that potential — through education, skill, and recognition.

📚

Education for All

We partner with underserved communities to provide access to quality education, from foundational literacy to digital skills that open doors in the modern economy.

🛠️

Skill Development

Practical, industry-aligned vocational training programs that empower individuals — especially youth and women — to become economically self-reliant.

🏅

Recognition & Awards

We actively seek out and celebrate talent across arts, crafts, sports, academics, and innovation — giving exceptional individuals the recognition they deserve.

🤝

Community Upliftment

Building strong local networks and mentorship ecosystems so that success in one generation seeds opportunity for the next.

"Every child born in Bharat carries within them the seed of greatness. Our mission is to ensure that seed finds the right soil, sunlight, and sky to bloom."
— Embrace Bharat Foundation

What We Do

Our Programs

Six flagship initiatives addressing education, skills, and talent at every stage of life.

Partner With Us
Flagship

Kaushal Kendra

Vocational training centers in rural and semi-urban areas offering certified courses in carpentry, tailoring, electronics repair, digital literacy, and more — all free of cost.

🗓 Year-round · 6-week modules Enroll →
Education

Vidya Setu

Bridge education program for school dropouts, providing accelerated learning pathways to re-enter mainstream schooling or qualify for vocational training.

🧑‍🎓 Ages 10–24 Learn more →
Tech

Digital Bharat Disha

Intensive digital skills bootcamps — coding, data entry, social media for small businesses, and cybersecurity basics — designed for first-generation technology learners.

💻 Online + offline Apply →
Women

Shakti Udyam

Entrepreneurship and self-employment program for women, covering business basics, microfinance literacy, and access to our network of mentors and market linkages.

👩‍💼 Women 18–45 Register →
Arts

Kalakaar Pehchaan

A dedicated platform for artists, craftspeople, and performers from indigenous and folk traditions to receive training, exposure, and preservation support.

🎨 Open to all ages Participate →
Youth

Pratibha Scholarship

Annual merit-cum-means scholarships for academically exceptional students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, covering tuition, books, and mentorship for three years.

🎓 150 scholars/year Apply →

Pratibha Samman Award

Our highest honor — conferred upon individuals whose body of work has transformed lives

Selection Criteria

Minimum 10 years of consistent contribution in their chosen field, with verifiable community impact

Demonstrated commitment to knowledge transfer and mentoring the next generation

Recognized by at least two community institutions or peer bodies independently of EBF

Commitment to values of inclusivity, integrity, and service beyond personal gain
