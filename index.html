Pratibha Samman Awards

Celebrating the Spirit of Selfless Service

Social work is often a thankless job. At Embrace Bharat Foundation, we believe that recognizing those who dedicate their lives to social welfare is crucial for sustaining a compassionate society.

Every year, we host our flagship Pratibha Samman Ceremony to honor unsung heroes from rural and urban communities across the country who actively participate in uplifting their environment.

  • ✓
    Certificates of Excellence
    Awarded to local leaders and educators for exceptional community upliftment.
  • ✓
    The Embrace Bharat Medals
    Gold and Silver medals conferred upon those who show extraordinary bravery and dedication during disaster relief.
  • ✓
    Youth Welfare Scholarships
    Grants paired with certificates for young adults leading tech-based social innovations.
